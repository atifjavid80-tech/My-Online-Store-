WHAT WE DO

Match Making
A professional matchmaking service is a personalized dating service that connects clients with potential partners through a curated, human-driven process rather than a dating app algorithm. They typically involve in-depth consultation and assessment to understand a client's preferences, and then matchmakers use their own networks and databases to find out potential matches. Benefits include a discreet and personalized experience, and some services also offer coaching or dating advice.
Content modeling agencies
can refer to two different types of businesses: model and talent agencies that manage models and create for content creation; or digital content agencies that specialise in planning and servicing digital content; and all agencies manage talent for physical and digital content like advertisements, photo shoots, and social media campaigns.
The girlfriend experience,
often provided through an escort agency, is a popular service that involves more personal interaction than a traditional call girl offers. There is a focus on not just having sex, but also having more of a comprehensive experience.
Sugar dating agencies
are online platforms (websites and apps) that facilitate a transactional dating practice where a wealthier, typically older, individual provides financial assistance and gifts to support a "sugar baby". This is a mutually beneficial relationship that is largely an online phenomenon and has grown in visibility since the 2000s.
Traditional modeling agencies
act as intermediaries between models and clients, handling job bookings, contract negotiations, and career guidance in exchange for a commission. They find and develop talent by scouting for new faces and managing the careers of their models through various tasks like building portfolios and practicing skills. Some agencies have specific requirements like height for fashion models, but they also represent models for commercial work, which can be more inclusive.
